About Vé

Vé comes from the name of the Nordic God Vé who gave the ability of language to humans. This project was inspired by the skills of this heavenly force, as the aim of this project is to help language learners to acquire foreign languages.

Learning by Translating

By using the benefits of the digital age, Vé challenges your foreign language expertise by translating sentences from your favourite domain. Vé is currently available for English to Spanish with sentences from books, movies, legal texts and general conversations.
